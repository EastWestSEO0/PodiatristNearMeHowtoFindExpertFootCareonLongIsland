Types of Bunion Surgery

Osteotomy

The podiatrist cuts and realigns the bone to correct the deformity.

Exostectomy

The bunion is shaved off, often combined with soft tissue correction.

Arthrodesis

The joint is fused in severe cases, commonly associated with arthritis.

Lapidus Procedure

Used for unstable joints, involving fusion of the metatarsal bone.
